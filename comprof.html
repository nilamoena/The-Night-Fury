About us

The Night Fury is a social networking service based, with people who loves design in it to collaborate, help each other and create jobs. The website allows members to communicate each other, to create jobs around design, which can be accepted by another members and get a reward/money if they can finished perfectly. Even members can also browse jobs created by other members.
